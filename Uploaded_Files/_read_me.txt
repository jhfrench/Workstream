From the FAAD 4.0 RS (FAAD\documentation\release_4_0_0\FAAD_4_0_0_RS.docx):

	-The application shall be backed up on a monthly basis at the end of each month.
	-Monthly back-ups shall be retained for a period of one (1) year.
	-The application shall be backed up yearly at the end of each fiscal year: after September 30.
	-The yearly back-ups shall be retained for a period of ten (10) years.
